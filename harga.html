FAQ

    Is it really free?

    Yes! We are here to make your marriage and that of tens of thousands of other couples easier for free. Additional fees are only required if there are additions according to needs.
    Which package is right for me?

    You can use a calculator to calculate as needed. We recommend that you just register for the free package first so you can understand better.

    What is meant by invited guests, whatsapp?

    Invited guests are a quota for getting a unique code that can be used to RSVP and check in for each guest.

    WhatsApp is a quota for distributing personal invitations to guests. Sending invitations can be done automatically to all guests or one by one.
    How to pay?

    We offer a variety of conveniences. Payment can be made using various payment methods, such as credit cards, bank transfers, or e-Walet. The process is instant and safe!
